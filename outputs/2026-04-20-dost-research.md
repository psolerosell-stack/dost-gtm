## Company Snapshot

Dost is a Barcelona-founded (2021) AI-powered platform that automates end-to-end invoice and delivery note processing for mid-market finance teams in Spain and the UK. The company uses proprietary AI models (not generic OCR) to extract, match, and route AP and AR documents through a unified workflow — delivering 95% extraction accuracy and 80% admin cost reduction across 150+ enterprise customers. November 2025 was a significant inflection: £6M Series A led by Octopus Ventures, simultaneous London office launch, CEO Adam Barbera relocated from Barcelona.
