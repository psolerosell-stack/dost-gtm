## Copy Notes

**What to A/B test first:**
- Subject lines: curiosity (option A) vs. insight (option B) in Persona 1 and 5 — these have highest volume
- Touch 1 length: Persona 1 and 2 could test a shorter 3-sentence variant
- ERP specificity: test {{erp_system}} personalized vs. generic "your ERP" to see if specificity lifts response

**Language rules across all personas:**
- Never say "AI-powered" — say what it does (captures, extracts, matches, routes)
- Never say "seamless" or "streamline" — use specific outcomes
- Never open with "I hope this finds you well" or "My name is"
- Always name the specific ERP in touch 1 and 2 — specificity signals research, not spray-and-pray
- Verifactu can be added as a P.S. on all Spain-targeted touches: "P.S. If Verifactu compliance is also on your radar, Dost handles e-invoicing natively — happy to cover both in one call."
