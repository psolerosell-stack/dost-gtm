# Company Profile — Relay

## Company Overview

- **Name:** Relay
- **Domain:** relay.io
- **Product:** Workflow automation platform for RevOps and GTM teams at growth-stage B2B SaaS companies. Relay connects CRM, billing, and product — the handoffs, alerts, syncs, and routing logic that every ops team currently maintains by hand.
- **Key differentiator:** Native CRM depth (Salesforce and HubSpot) with no-code building. Teams that outgrew Zapier but don't want to involve engineering.
- **Stage:** Series B — $28M raised (Bessemer, February 2023)
- **Notable customers:** Watershed, Ashby, Drata, Navan (ops team only)
- **Competitors:** Zapier Teams, Workato, Tray.io, custom engineering
- **Headcount:** 85 people, 22 in GTM

## Deal Characteristics

- **Sales cycle:** 32 days median (enterprise: 60–90 days)
- **ACV range:** $24,000 – $180,000
- **Deal motion:** AE-led evaluation with RevOps technical champion. Economic buyer is typically CRO or VP Sales who approves the budget but defers to the champion on the evaluation.
- **Self-serve path:** Free tier exists, but conversion from free to paid requires a sales conversation at the $24k+ price point. PLG provides awareness; it does not drive revenue directly.
- **Expansion:** Seat-based expansion within existing accounts is the primary growth motion. Typical expansion 9–12 months post-close as teams standardize workflows on Relay.

## Target Personas

- **Primary buyer:** Head of RevOps / Sr. RevOps Manager — owns the tooling budget, lives in Salesforce, measures themselves on pipeline data quality and process efficiency.
- **Economic buyer:** CRO / VP Sales — approves budget, cares about rep efficiency and forecast accuracy, not workflow automation specifically.
- **Technical evaluator:** GTM Engineer / Sales Ops Analyst — runs the proof of concept, cares about API quality, integration reliability, migration path from Zapier.

**Anti-ICP:** Sub-50 employees (no dedicated ops function), 2,000+ employees (have internal engineering capacity to build), companies using Workato as core infrastructure (migration cost too high), agencies and services firms (wrong ops motion).

## Product

### Core: Workflow Automation
No-code workflow builder with native Salesforce and HubSpot connectors. Teams automate: account routing, deal stage alerts, data sync between tools, lifecycle management, and custom scoring triggers.

Relay-specific differentiator: "Human-in-the-loop" steps that pause an automation for manual approval before continuing. Zapier and Workato treat automation as binary — either fully automated or fully manual. Relay supports a middle ground that enterprise RevOps teams actually need.

### Key integrations
Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Intercom, Slack, Notion, Jira. Full list at relay.io/integrations.
