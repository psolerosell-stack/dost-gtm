# Campaign Brief: Series B + RevOps Hire — Tier 2

**Created:** 2024-03-01
**Owner:** Marcus Webb (SDR Lead)
**Status:** Live — launched March 4, 2024

---

## Trigger Logic

**Signal combination:** Series B announced in last 60 days AND active RevOps hire (last 30 days)
Both signals must be present. Either alone goes to the standard single-signal campaign.

**Suppression:** Existing customers, active opportunities, contacted in last 60 days, any unsubscribed contact.

**Estimated list size:** 41 accounts matching criteria as of March 1. Refreshed weekly — expect 8–12 new accounts per week as new raises get announced.

---

## Target Audience

- **ICP Tier:** 2
- **Firmographic filter:** 100–300 employees, B2B SaaS, Series B raised in last 60 days
- **Persona:** Head of RevOps / Senior RevOps Manager (the new hire specifically, or the most senior RevOps person if the hire is for a report)
- **Account status:** Cold — no prior contact

**Why this combination?**
Series B alone means budget and growth pressure but not necessarily urgency around tooling. The RevOps hire on top of it means someone is actively auditing and rebuilding. The combination creates a specific, datable moment — it's not "they might eventually have this problem," it's "they are in the middle of this right now."
