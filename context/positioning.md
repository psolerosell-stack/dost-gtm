Last updated: 2026-04-20

## Core Positioning Statement

Dost is the AI-powered invoice automation platform for mid-market finance teams in Europe. Unlike generic OCR tools or enterprise AP suites, Dost uses proprietary AI trained specifically on complex financial documents — delivering 95% extraction accuracy and unifying AP and AR in a single workflow that finance teams can actually run without IT.

**The mechanism:** Dost's AI is trained on millions of real invoice and delivery note formats — not a generic document model. This means it handles edge cases (handwritten fields, non-standard supplier formats, multi-page delivery notes) that break OCR-based competitors. The result is a system that processes invoices accurately from day one, not after months of training.

## What Not to Say

**Don't position as "OCR software"** — Dost uses proprietary AI models, not OCR. OCR implies brittle, rules-based extraction. Our differentiator is that the AI learns document formats rather than requiring templates. Saying "OCR" undersells the technology and invites comparison to Rossum/Hypatos on technical terms.

**Don't lead with "AP automation" in UK outbound** — the category term is less established in the UK. Lead with the outcome: "your finance team processes invoices in minutes, not days." Let them name the category.

**Don't promise 100% automation on day one** — 95% accuracy is accurate and impressive. Claiming full automation sets expectations for a perfect system. The honest message: 95% automated from go-live, edge cases handled via exception workflow.
